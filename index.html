Bienvenida / Bienvenido

Serving up a fiesta on wheels, our taco truck brings the vibrant flavors of traditional street food straight to your neighborhood.

  • Huevos con Chorizo

    Huevos rotos con chorizo is a Spanish dish of fried potatoes topped with a runny egg, often with chorizo or cured ham.

  • Entomatadas

    Traditional mexican dish covered in our homemeade tomato sauce

  • Tlayudas

    Tlayudas are Mexican street food, a sort of pizza from the southern state of Oaxacas.

  • Authentic Street Tacos

    Street tacos are typically served on small, soft corn tortillas and are filled with simple yet flavorful ingredients such as grilled meat, onions, cilantro, and salsa.

  • Cocktel de Camaron

    Coctel de camarones, or Mexican shrimp cocktail, is a refreshing dish of shrimp, spices, and vegetables

Tacos Galore in Town!

Eva Gutierrez is a dynamic and enthusiastic business owner in Arlington, WA, who has successfully brought a slice of her rich Hispanic heritage to the local community through her venture, Tacos La Más Fregona. Immersed in the vibrant traditions of Mexican cuisine from an early age, Eva saw an opportunity to share the authentic tastes and recipes that she grew up with, right in the heart of Arlington. Determined to fill the gap in the market for genuine Mexican flavors, she launched Tacos La Más Fregona, a taco truck that quickly became a local favorite, known for its irresistible tacos, made with love and a deep respect for tradition.
